**Last Updated:** January 28, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Camorent Vendor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Camorent Vendor is a business-to-business (B2B) platform designed for camera equipment rental vendors to manage their rental business, bookings, inventory, and financial settlements.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information that you provide to us:

**Account Information:**
- Full name (first and last name)
- Mobile phone number (10-digit Indian format)
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- Date of birth (for identity verification purposes)

**Business Information:**
- Company/Business name

- Vendor role classification (buyer/owner/admin)

**Identity Documents:**

### 1.2 Operational and Transactional Data

**Booking and Rental Information:**
- Order details (order names, SKU IDs, product descriptions)
- Rental dates (start and end dates)
- Crew member selections and quantities
- Equipment rental information (quantities, prices)

**Financial Information:**
- Revenue statistics
- Amount due and pending settlements

- Settlement request details

**Images and Media:**
- Product condition photos taken during pickup
- Product condition photos taken during return

### 1.3 Device and Technical Information

**Automatically Collected Information:**
- Device token (for push notifications)
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Network connection status
- Error logs and crash reports (when enabled)

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

**Location Data:** We explicitly do NOT collect or track your geographical location. Location permissions are blocked in the App.

**Audio Recordings:** While the audio permission may be enabled for future features, we do not currently record audio.

**Browsing History:** We do not track your web browsing or activities outside the App.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 4.1 Encryption

**In Transit:**
- All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption (TLS/SSL)
- API requests are secured with Bearer token authentication

**At Rest:**
- Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain using Expo SecureStore
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before storage
- Sensitive data uses device-level encryption (Android Keystore, iOS Keychain)

### 4.2 Access Controls

- Role-based access control (buyer/owner/admin permissions)
- Multi-factor authentication via OTP for critical operations (pickup/return)
- Token-based session management with automatic expiration
- Refresh token rotation for enhanced security

### 4.3 Data Storage

**On Your Device:**
- Secure storage for authentication tokens (encrypted)
- Local cache for non-sensitive user profile data (AsyncStorage)
- Temporary storage for images before upload

**On Our Servers:**
- Encrypted database storage
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Firewall protection and intrusion detection systems
- Access logs and monitoring

### 4.4 Security Best Practices

- Input validation to prevent injection attacks
- Error boundaries to prevent app crashes from exposing sensitive data
- Rate limiting on API endpoints to prevent abuse
- Automatic logout after extended inactivity

**Important Notice:** While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

**Account Data:** Retained for the duration of your active account, plus 7 years after account closure for tax and legal compliance (as per Indian Income Tax Act)
